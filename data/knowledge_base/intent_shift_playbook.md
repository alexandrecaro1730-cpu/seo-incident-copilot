# Intent Shift Playbook

Business objective: recover visibility when Google changes the preferred result format for a valuable query.

When a commercial landing page loses rankings to listicles, review category pages, buyer guides, or competitor comparison pages, do not only rewrite the landing page. Build or refresh a supporting comparison asset, add comparison modules to the landing page, and strengthen internal links from informational pages to the commercial page.

Evidence signals:
- top results change from vendor landing pages to listicles or review pages
- new SERP includes words like best, top, comparison, alternatives, reviews
- the affected landing page lacks comparison sections, alternatives, or buyer-guide intent coverage

Recommended actions:
- create or update a comparison/listicle asset
- add a comparison module to the landing page
- add FAQ and alternatives sections
- internally link from the new asset to the commercial landing page
- re-check rankings after the next crawl cycle
