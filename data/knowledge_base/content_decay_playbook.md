# Content Decay Playbook

Business objective: preserve rankings when competitors improve content depth, trust, and conversion coverage.

If SERP intent is stable but competitors add pricing, ROI calculators, integrations, case studies, comparison tables, FAQ, schema, or updated 2026 messaging, the issue is likely content decay or competitive content improvement.

Evidence signals:
- the top SERP remains mostly commercial
- affected page is old or thin compared with competitors
- page lacks pricing, comparison table, security, integrations, FAQ, or case studies
- internal links are lower than comparable winning pages

Recommended actions:
- refresh the landing page with missing modules
- add FAQPage or SoftwareApplication schema where appropriate
- update screenshots, integrations, and use cases
- improve internal links from supporting content
- compare conversion impact after deployment
